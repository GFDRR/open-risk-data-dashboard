ABOUT


The Open Data for Resilience Index is an online tool to track and assess the openness of key data related to disaster risk management (DRM) across the world.

The Open Data for Resilience Index is developed and managed by the Global Facility for Disaster Reduction and Recovery (GFDRR).

Users of the Open Data for Resilience Index can provide feedback through the feedback form available on every page of the website.

Acknowledgement

The Open Data for Resilience Index had been developed and is being maintained with the help of CIMA Foundation, Global Earthquake Model and Deltares.

Disclaimer

Information available on the Open Data for Resilience Index are collected through a crowdsourced effort by registered users and then reviewed by a team of DRM specialists. Although it aims to provide accurate and up to date information, the Open Data for Resilience Index cannot guarantee the accuracy and freshness of all information related to key datasets available on the website.
